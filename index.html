Biography

Kevin De Broyer (Halle, april 1st, 1993) is a Belgian artist. He is mostly known as a landscape painter . He currently lives and works in Blankenberge and Ghent. He was born into a family with 1 sister, 4 brothers, and a lovely mother called Marleen.

He first started painting when he joined the academy of fine arts in Bruges, where he graduated in 2015. After that he joined LUCA school of arts in 2016, where he is still studying.

His works is mostly realistic with abstract elements, though he never loses the realistic parts. Kevin explained "For me a good abstract painter needs maturity. I'm not ready yet. For me a landscape is a medium to show my emotions and love of colour without making something completely abstract."

His work is inspired by lots of artists; mostly the abstract impressionists and abstract expressionists, but also painters like Richard Diebenkorn, Wayne Thiebaud and David Hockney.
